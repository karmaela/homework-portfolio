About Michelle

I am a Sydney based Creative currently employed as an Apparel Graphic Designer/Content Manager. I also freelance in Graphic Design, Visual Art, Content Management and Fashion Design. I have an honours degree in Fashion and Textiles design, currently undergoing training in Front End Website Development. In pursuit of transforming hobbie to employment, I have set up this website to share my work and love for creativity.

About Karmaela

Karmaela is my contribution to the world wide web, drawing inspiration from artists, designers, musicians and other inmates of society. Other vendors for my imagination include: pop culture, science fiction and fantasy; grounded by societal influence and humans from all walks of life.
